Project Proposal

Project Theme

The theme for the project will be a travelogue of vacation photos from various exotic destinations in Southeast Asia.

Ideal Audience

The ideal audience for the project will be travellers, tourists, students, or families intending to vacation in Southeast Asia.

Brief Description of Pages

About Us- Information on the author of the website and a brief description of the content offered on the website.

Contact Us- Contact information of the author of the website and best way to reach him/her.

Countries- Photographs and information on various travel destinations, sights, and recommended accommodations organized by country. As well as information on local culture, cuisine, and languages.

Popular Destinations- Photographs and descriptions of the most popular tourist destinations in Southeast Asia. Not organized by country, but by popularity.

Photo Catalogue- A collection of all the photographs available on a single page, organized by country.

News- Important regional news from Southeast Asia that may affect travel plans or well-being of travellers and tourists.

Prime Objective of Website

The main objective of this website is to showcase the natural and cultural beauty of Southeast Asia. This website may also be used by potential travellers or tourists to plan their trip or learn more about the various local sightseeing destinations.

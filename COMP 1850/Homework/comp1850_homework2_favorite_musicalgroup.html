The Killers

The Killers are an American rock band formed in Las Vegas, Nevada, in 2001 by members: Brandon Flowers (lead vocals, keyboards, bass) and Dave Keuning (guitar, backing vocals), Mark Stoermer (bass, guitar, backing vocals) and Ronnie Vannucci, Jr. (drums, percussion)

They are seen as one of the biggest rock bands of the 21st century, and the most successful rock band to ever emerge from the state of Nevada, having sold an estimated 22 million records worldwide. They have also achieved worldwide success as a live band, performing in over 50 countries and on six continents, headlining venues such as Madison Square Garden and Wembley Stadium.

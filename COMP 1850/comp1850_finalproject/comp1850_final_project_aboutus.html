About the Author:

Brian was born in Taipei, Taiwan. His family immigrated to Canada when he was 10 years old. Growing up in Vancouver, he enjoyed the wonderful outdoor landscape of British Columbia. However, he has always wanted to expand his horizons by traveling the world. Brian fell in love with the scenery, culture, and people of Southeast Asia when he visited in 2016.

One of his most memorable experiences was watching the sunrise from the ancient temple complex of Wat Arun in Bangkok, Thailand.

About the Website:

This website showcases some of the beautiful sceneries found in Southeast Asia. Its intended audience are travelers, tourists, students, or families looking to vacation or explore the landscapes and cityscapes of this incredible part of the world.

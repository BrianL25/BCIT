Here you will find important regional news from Southeast Asia that may affect travel plans or well-being of travelers and tourists.

October 22, 2017

Burial of the Late Thai King will take place from 25th to 29th of October

If you intend to visit Thailand during those days please take note that Bangkok will be extremely crowded and the royal palace and surrounding temples will be closed until the 30th. Most stores and restaurants will be closed during this time.

News Link: Reuters Article

October 21, 2017

Avoid all travel in the Mindanao Region and vicinity of the Philippines

Global Affairs Canada advises against all travel to the Mindanao Region of the Philippines due to the serious threat of terrorist attacks and kidnapping.
